## âš¡ Notes & Tips

1. **PowerShell is object-based**, not text-based like bash. Piping (`|`) passes objects instead of strings.
2. **Case-insensitive**: Cmdlets and parameters in PowerShell are not case-sensitive.
3. **Flags differ**: Linux flags like `ls -l` or `rm -rf` may not work directly in PowerShell.
4. **Cross-platform**: PowerShell 7+ runs on Windows, Linux, and macOS.
